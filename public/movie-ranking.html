The Best Movies According to Aditya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Lagaan

It successfully blends sports, drama, and patriotism, and the combination of cricket, culture, and historical backdrop struck a chord with audiences.

3 Idiots

Directed by Rajkumar Hirani, "3 Idiots" is a comedy-drama that not only entertained but also delivered a powerful message about the Indian education system and societal pressures.

Dangal

The film showcases the journey of these young women as they break stereotypes and achieve excellence in wrestling.
